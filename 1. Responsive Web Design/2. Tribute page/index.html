  • born in Craiova, on 15 February
  • attended the primary school in Craiova
  • continued primary school at Protodeacon Iosif Barac's School
  • was enlisted at the Romanian Gymnasium from Schei-Braşov, a gymnasium founded in 1850 through his uncle Ioan Popazu's endeavour
  • attended the first grade at the Academic Gymnasium, which was an addendum of the Theresianum Academy for foreigners
  • sent an essay signed with the name Aureliu to the Transylvania Gazette in order to publish some of his translations from Jean Paul's works
  • got his license in Philology and Philosophy at Sorbonne due to the acknowledgment of his doctorate from Giessen
  • founded "Junimea" alongside his friends I. Negruzzi, Petre P. Carp, V. Pogor and Th.Rosetti
  • he obtained his Law Licence in Paris, on the thesis "Du régime dotal" ("On Dowery Law")
  • he was assigned as a substitute lawyer at the Law Court, then he became an attorney
  • published his first critical review, "About Romanian poetry" in "Convorbiri literare"
  • publishes the article "About the reform of public education" in "Convorbiri literare", an unfinished research, yet it represents his ideologies during that age
  • his parliamentary career starts, moment that will have engaged his life
  • published the article "Poets and critics", a polemical response to Vasile Alecsandri's attempts to join the field of literary critics
  • retires from teaching, under the plea of "old age"
  • is named prime minister and foreign minister
  • partakes in the Romanian Crown Council, pronouncing himself for neutrality
  • dies at Bucharest at 77 due to heart disease
RO: "Ideea sau obiectul exprimat prin poezie este totdeauna un simţământ sau o pasiune, şi niciodată o cugetare exclusiv intelectuală sau care se ţine de tărâmul ştiinţific, fie în teorie, fie în aplicarea practică."
EN: "The idea or the expressed object through poetry is always a feeling or a passion, and never an exclusive intelectual insight or one that holds to the scientifical field, be it theory, or in practical application."
